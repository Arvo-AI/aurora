# Social Media Strategy

## Inspiration

- [Revyl on X](https://x.com/tryrevyl)

## Schedule

- **Content creation**: Every morning until morning meeting
- **Posting**: Every 2 days minimum

## Platforms

- **LinkedIn** — Standard posts
- **X** — Standard posts
- **Reddit** — Insightful, discussion-oriented posts

## Accounts & Roles

### Company Account
- Create a professional-looking company account
- Get the professional/verified badge
- Reserved for big updates only — post occasionally, not daily

### Employee Posters
- 1–2 employees post under the company account day-to-day
- These are the regular content creators driving the cadence above

## Content Pillars

### 1. Industry Commentary
- General developer issues in our area (observability, incident response, on-call pain)
- Post about major outages happening that day — timely, relevant takes

### 2. Original Research
- Aggregate data on uptime across top 100 tech companies
- Surface interesting reliability/infrastructure trends
- Genuinely insightful — data-driven, not fluff

### 3. Product Demos
- Feature walkthroughs and short demos
- Use [Trupeer](https://app.trupeer.ai/) for AI-assisted demo video creation
